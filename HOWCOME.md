## 🇬🇧 English

### The Context

In many engineering and construction environments, project schedules are maintained using enterprise-grade planning platforms. These systems are powerful and comprehensive, but accessing schedule data often requires licensed software, full installations, and controlled environments.

For high-level review, validation, or analytical inspection, this level of dependency may not always be practical.

### The Constraint

In certain operational situations:

- Only exported schedule files were available  
- Access to licensed planning software was limited  
- Review needed to be performed on shared or restricted machines  
- A lightweight analytical interface was required  

Opening a full enterprise system merely to inspect activities, dates, or relationships was inefficient.

### The Insight

Most schedule analysis tasks rely on structured information:

- Activities  
- Start and finish dates  
- Logical relationships  
- Float  
- Baseline comparison  

If exported schedule data can be parsed, the analytical layer can be reconstructed independently — without replicating the original enterprise environment.

### The Decision

ProVI AnalyXER was developed as a lightweight, browser-based analytical tool.

It is not intended to replace commercial planning platforms.  
Instead, it serves as:

- A fast schedule viewer  
- A portable analytical utility  
- A management-friendly interface  
- A schedule health inspection dashboard  

All operating fully client-side.

### The Philosophy

Project data should remain:

- Inspectable  
- Portable  
- Transparent  
- Independent from unnecessary operational constraints  

ProVI AnalyXER is a practical response to workflow limitations — designed to complement, not compete with, enterprise systems.

## 🇨🇳 中文

### 背景

在工程与建设领域，项目进度通常通过企业级计划系统进行管理。这类系统功能强大且全面，但访问进度数据往往需要授权许可、完整的软件安装以及受控的运行环境。

对于高层审阅、技术验证或数据分析而言，这种依赖有时并不高效。

### 现实限制

在某些实际工作场景中：

- 仅获得导出的进度文件  
- 无法访问具备许可的软件  
- 需要在受限或共享设备上进行查看  
- 需要轻量级的分析工具  

仅为查看活动或逻辑关系而启动完整的企业系统，效率较低。

### 核心思路

大多数进度分析基于结构化数据，例如：

- 活动  
- 开始与完成日期  
- 逻辑关系  
- 浮时（Float）  
- 基准对比  

如果能够解析导出的数据文件，就可以在不依赖原系统环境的情况下重建分析层。

### 开发决策

ProVI AnalyXER 因此被开发为一个基于浏览器的轻量级分析工具。

它并非用于替代商业计划系统，  
而是作为：

- 快速进度查看工具  
- 便携式分析工具  
- 面向管理层的界面  
- 项目健康检查仪表板  

完全在客户端运行。

### 理念

项目数据应当：

- 可被审阅  
- 可被携带  
- 保持透明  
- 尽量减少不必要的系统依赖  

ProVI AnalyXER 是对实际工作流程限制的工程化回应，旨在补充，而非取代企业系统。
